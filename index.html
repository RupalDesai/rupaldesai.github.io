We are registered under NDIS Provider in Northern Territory

At Northern Support Services, we stand out as more than just an NDIS provider. We are your partners in navigating the path to a better tomorrow. Our unique approach, grounded in respect, empathy, and empowerment, sets us apart. We understand that each person's needs are unique, so we tailor our services to fit the goals and aspirations of the individuals and families we serve.

A shared vision drives our dedicated team: to enhance the quality of life for our clients through innovative, responsive, and client-centered care. We pride ourselves on our strong local presence and deep understanding of the Northern Territory's unique challenges and opportunities. This enables us to deliver support that is not only effective but truly meaningful.

Our Services

Daily Living Support

We offer personalized help with daily activities to ensure your routines run smoothly. Our team, known for their respect and professionalism, assistance with personal care, meal preparation, and other daily tasks, always respecting your privacy and personal boundaries. We are here to support you in maintaining your independence and quality of life.

Social, Economic, and Community Engagement

Northern Support Services is more than just about activities; it's about connection. We help you connect with your community and engage in social and economic activities. By facilitating your involvement in local events, programs, and opportunities, we enable you to build meaningful relationships and feel a sense of belonging.

Enhanced Community Participation

We focus on increasing social and community involvement by providing tailored strategies and support. Our team will guide you through joining social groups or participating in community events, ensuring you feel included and enjoy a more fulfilling life.

Respite Care

Caring for a loved one can be demanding, and everyone needs a break sometimes. Our respite care services provide temporary relief for primary caregivers, ensuring your loved ones receive professional care and support. At the same time, you take time to rest and recharge.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Our services are designed to help you live independently, whether in a shared or individual living arrangement. We offer ongoing support with daily tasks, personal care, and community engagement, ensuring you have the assistance you need to thrive in your living environment.
