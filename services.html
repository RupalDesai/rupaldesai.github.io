Services Offered

Our Services

At Northern Support Services, our unwavering dedication is to provide a comprehensive range of support designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Here's how we can assist you:

Daily Living Support

  • We offer personalized help with daily activities to ensure your routines run smoothly.
  • Our team, known for their respect and professionalism, assistance with personal care, meal preparation, and other daily tasks, always respecting your privacy and personal boundaries.
  • We are here to support you in maintaining your independence and quality of life.

Social, Economic, and Community Engagement

  • Northern Support Services is more than just about activities; it's about connection.
  • We help you connect with your community and engage in social and economic activities.
  • By facilitating your involvement in local events, programs, and opportunities, we enable you to build meaningful relationships and feel a sense of belonging.

Enhanced Community Participation

  • We focus on increasing social and community involvement by providing tailored strategies and support.
  • Our team will guide you through joining social groups or participating in community events, ensuring you feel included and enjoy a more fulfilling life.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

  • Dedicated support to help you live independently in your home or a shared living arrangement.
  • Our SIL services include personalized support plans, 24/7 assistance, skill development, and safe, comfortable living environments.
  • We focus on enhancing your independence while providing the necessary support to manage daily life effectively.

Respite Care

  • Caring for a loved one can be demanding, and everyone needs a break sometimes.
  • Our respite care services provide temporary relief for primary caregivers, ensuring your loved ones receive professional care and support.
  • At the same time, you take time to rest and recharge.

At Northern Support Services, we believe everyone deserves to live independently and with dignity. Our services are designed to help you achieve just that, offering the support you need to live confidently and comfortably in your home. We are here to empower you to live life on your terms. Please feel free to contact us for more information on the services we provide.
